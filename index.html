Bank problem

Por otro lado, los altos costos y centralización de la banca tradicional limitan los servicios transaccionales a nivel global.

The unbanked are forced to have a domestic and informal economy, without access to the security provided by the financial system, or to the possibility of using savings instruments. On the other hand, the high costs and centralization of traditional banking limit transactional services globally.
